# Product Marketing Context — ZiggySitters.com
*Last updated: 2026-02-07*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Find trusted pet sitters in New Zealand
**What it does:** Marketplace connecting pet owners with verified local pet sitters for in-home care, dog walking, and overnight stays.
**Category:** Pet Services Marketplace
**Business model:** Commission on bookings (TBD) or subscription

## Target Audience
**Target customers:**
- Pet owners in NZ (dogs, cats primarily)
- Busy professionals, travelers, holidaymakers
- Auckland metro initially, expanding to Wellington/Christchurch

**Decision-makers:** Pet owner booking care
**Primary use case:** Need someone to care for pet while traveling or working

**Jobs to be done:**
1. Find a trustworthy person to care for my pet
2. Avoid stressful kennels (pets stay home or with sitter)
3. Get daily updates while away

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Hard to find reliable pet sitters, kennels stress pets out, friends aren't always available
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Kennels: Stressful for pets, expensive, impersonal
- Facebook groups: Unvetted, unreliable, no accountability
- Friends/family: Can't always help, feel guilty asking

**Cost to them:** Anxiety while away, limited travel options, pet stress
**Emotional tension:** Guilt about leaving pet, worry about care quality, trust issues

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** PetCloud NZ, MadPaws (AU), local pet sitting agencies
**Secondary:** Boarding kennels, vet boarding
**Indirect:** Asking friends/family

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Verified NZ sitters (police checks)
- In-home care (pet stays comfortable)
- Daily photo updates
- Local NZ focus (not AU-first)

**Why customers choose us:** Trust + convenience + pet stays happy

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "How do I know the sitter is trustworthy?" | All sitters are verified with ID checks. Reviews from real pet owners. |
| "What if something goes wrong?" | 24/7 support, vet line available, insurance coverage. |
| "Cheaper to ask a friend" | Friends aren't always available. We're here when you need us. |

**Anti-persona:**
- People with family nearby who can help
- Farm/rural properties (sitters are urban-focused)
- Exotic pet owners (initial focus: dogs/cats)

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:** Kennel traumatized pet, friend canceled last minute, Facebook sitter was flaky
**Pull:** Verified profiles, reviews, convenience, daily updates
**Habit:** "I always use the same kennel" / "My mum looks after him"
**Anxiety:** "Is a stranger in my home safe? Will they actually care?"

## Customer Language
**Problem (verbatim):**
- "My dog hates kennels"
- "I feel so guilty leaving her"
- "I just want to know she's okay while I'm away"

**Solution (verbatim):**
- "It's like having a friend look after your pet, but reliable"
- "The daily photos were amazing"

**Use:** pet sitter, dog sitting, cat sitter, pet care, trusted, verified, in-home
**Avoid:** kennel, boarding (we're the alternative)

## Brand Voice
**Tone:** Warm, caring, trustworthy
**Personality:** The reliable, animal-loving neighbor you wish you had

## Proof Points

**Testimonials:** None yet

## Goals
**Business goal:** First 10 bookings
**Conversion action:** Complete booking
**Current metrics:** ~20 site visitors, 0 bookings (chicken-egg: need sitters first)
